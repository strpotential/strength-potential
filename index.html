Om meg

Mitt navn er Jonatan Dam. Jeg tar en BSc i fysioterapi, og er sertifisert personlig trener gjennom Strength System Online Certification, level 1 og 2. Trening er noe som har en spesiell plass i hjertet mitt. Når jeg var 12 år gammel, ble jeg diagnotisert med kronisk utmattelsessyndrom. Jeg hadde redusert funksjon i dagliglivet i over 5 år, og vet hvordan det føles å være på bunnen. Dette gir meg ekstra verdsettelse for hvordan man kan bygge kroppen- og sinnet opp igjen.

Denne erfaringen har vært drivkraften bak karrieren min som personlig trener. Jeg drar nytte av erfaringer fra flere år som personlig trener, samt erfaringer som trener- og spiller for Sandnes Øst Rugbyklubb, som konkurrerer på nasjonal basis. Disse erfaringene lar meg hjelpe deg å oppnå ditt fulle potensiale, enten du aldri har rørt en vekt, eller har trent i flere år.

Hvem har jeg hjulpet med denne erfaringen?

Mari

Mari

Before starting at S&C I attended group sessions at a traditional fitness center. It was good for a while, but I started to realize that I had gotten the most I could out of it. Starting at S&C with Jonatan as my coach really changed things for me.

I wanted to join the military and was afraid that I would not be able to meet the admission requirements. In order to develop both strength and endurance, I joined my friend for a test-session at S&C, and I never looked back. After training at S&C for a while, Jonatan started as a coach there. In many ways, that was the best thing that ever happened to my training in terms of progression. After getting to know my current limitations, Jonatan wrote a program for me. Thanks to that, both my strength and motivation reach a new level.

In the beginning I was afraid of not being strong enough, not being able to meet the expectations or not being able to do certain exercises that he would give me. I soon found out that this was unnecessary to worry about. Jonatan is really down to earth, and has a truly comfortable charisma. Somehow, even if I was unhappy with my own performance, he made me feel successful.

During my time training with Jonatan, I have had a fast progression and have become much stronger in various ways over a relatively short period of time. In addition to being my coach, Jonatan has also become my friend. This has increased the joy of training, and made the hard work more fun. If you want someone to push you, and expand your limits, whilst being patient, respectful and aware of your conditions and ambitions, I would recommend Jonatan as a coach. He has a wide base of knowledge, and a drive to help people get the most out of their training.

PS: The admission requirements in the military were a piece of cake following Jonatan’s coaching.

Christer

Christer

I've worked as a personal trainer for five years, and feel I'm pretty knowledgeable. I talked a lot with Jonatan, and quickly understood how insightful he was in regards to strength training. I let him coach me, and quickly understood how far behind I actually was education-wise.

In an informal, respectful, and fun way he shared what he knew, and I ended up being a better trainer and got stronger. I learned how important programming is, and how many different ways there are of doing it. Jonatan is the kind of guy who speaks his mind, with no fear of being wrong. If your attitude around training is weak, he'll tell you, but not in a mean way. He'll help you gain perspective and train you in how to think to enjoy the training mentally, which leads to you growing physically.

I've seen him inspire people in all walks of life, me included. He's stern, but kind, and will always listen if you try to bail on an exercise, just make sure it's a legitimate excuse. He cares for his clients, and about their goals. Handing the reigns over to Jonatan, will let you turn your mind off, and simply follow his directions.

I've always believed in brutal honestly when it comes to training, and I've seen the glorious results it bears. Jonatan seems to agree, and has helped a lot of people dig themselves out of deep tunnels and build better lives. This is a quality trainer, who really knows what he's doing. Don't let this offer pass, it's unlikely you'll find a better one.
